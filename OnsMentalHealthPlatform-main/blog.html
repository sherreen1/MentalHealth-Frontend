Mood disorders

A mood disorder is a mental health condition that affects a person's feelings, thoughts, and behavior. People with a mood disorder experience large swings in their mood. They may feel extremely sad and hopeless at times, and at other times they may feel overly happy and hyperactive.

Obsessive-compulsive disorder

Compulsions are behaviors that the sufferer performs in an attempt to get rid of the anxiety or distress caused by the obsessions. They may include washing hands frequently, checking things over and over, or arranging things in a certain way. These thoughts and behaviors cause severe distress and interfere with daily life.
